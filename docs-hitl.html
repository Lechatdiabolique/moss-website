Human-in-the-loop

High-risk actions can pause for a person. Send them to the gate, then approve or deny from your own UI or the Console.

POST/v1/gateHold an action for human approval.
GET/v1/approvals/pendingList actions awaiting approval.
POST/v1/approve/{id}Approve a held action. The approval is signed.
POST/v1/deny/{id}Deny a held action.

Flow

  • An action evaluates to HOLD, or you send it straight to /v1/gate.
  • It appears in /v1/approvals/pending.
  • A reviewer approves or denies. The decision is signed and attributed to the reviewer.
Every approval and denial is recorded with who decided and when, as signed evidence.
